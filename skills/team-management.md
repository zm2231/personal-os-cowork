# Team Management

## Purpose
Lead, grow, and optimize high-performing teams by implementing effective people management practices, fostering collaboration, and driving team performance and satisfaction.

## Capabilities

### 🎯 Tier 1: Foundation (Session 21+)
**Team Fundamentals & Onboarding**

Build and support effective team foundations:

- **Team Charter**: Define team mission, vision, values, and working agreements
- **Role Clarity**: Create clear role descriptions and expectations for each team member
- **Onboarding Process**: Design comprehensive onboarding for new team members
- **Team Rhythm**: Establish regular meetings, check-ins, and communication cadence
- **Performance Baselines**: Set initial performance expectations and goals
- **Team Health Monitoring**: Implement basic team health checks and feedback loops

**Example:**
```
"I'm a new manager of a 5-person engineering team. Help me:
1. Create a team charter with mission, vision, and working agreements
2. Define clear roles for each engineer
3. Design a 30-60-90 day onboarding plan for new hires
4. Set up regular meeting cadence (daily standup, weekly sprint, monthly retro)
5. Establish initial performance expectations"
```

---

### 🚀 Tier 2: Advanced (Session 21+)
**Team Growth & Performance Optimization**

Lead team development and optimize performance:

- **Performance Management**: Implement regular performance reviews, feedback cycles, and goal setting
- **Skill Development**: Create personalized development plans and growth opportunities
- **Team Dynamics**: Facilitate team building, resolve conflicts, and improve collaboration
- **Succession Planning**: Identify high-potential team members and prepare future leaders
- **Retention Strategies**: Implement engagement initiatives and career progression paths
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration**: Foster relationships with other teams and stakeholders
- **Team Metrics**: Track performance, productivity, and satisfaction metrics

**Example:**
```
"Our team is growing to 10 people and I need to scale our management practices. Help me:
1. Implement a quarterly performance review process
2. Create individual development plans aligned with career goals
3. Address a conflict between two senior team members
4. Identify and mentor 2 high-potential engineers for future leadership
5. Design engagement initiatives to reduce turnover
6. Set up metrics to track team health and performance"
```

---

### 🌟 Tier 3: Anticipatory (Session 21+)
**Strategic Team Leadership**

Drive organizational impact through exceptional team leadership:

- **Organizational Design**: Design team structures that scale with business needs
- **Culture Transformation**: Shape team culture that aligns with organizational values
- **Talent Strategy**: Develop long-term talent acquisition and retention strategies
- **Change Leadership**: Lead teams through organizational changes and transformations
- **Team Autonomy**: Build self-organizing teams with high decision-making authority
- **Diversity & Inclusion**: Create inclusive environments that attract and retain diverse talent
- **Leadership Development**: Build a pipeline of future leaders within the team
- **Executive Alignment**: Align team strategy with organizational goals and executive priorities

**Example:**
```
"I'm leading a 20-person organization through a major transformation. Help me:
1. Redesign our team structure to support new strategic objectives
2. Shape our culture to embrace innovation and accountability
3. Build a 3-year talent strategy that supports growth from 20 to 50 people
4. Lead the team through a significant organizational change
5. Transition to self-organizing squads with product ownership
6. Develop a D&I program that increases representation by 30%
7. Create a leadership development program that produces 3 new managers annually
8. Align all team OKRs with executive strategic priorities"
```

## Best Practices

### ✅ DO:
- Build trust: Be transparent, authentic, and follow through on commitments
- Provide regular feedback: Give continuous feedback, not just during reviews
- Develop your people: Prioritize team growth and career development
- Celebrate wins: Recognize and appreciate team achievements
- Lead by example: Model the behavior you expect from your team
- Listen actively: Seek to understand before being understood
- Empower decisions: Give team members autonomy and decision-making authority
- Focus on outcomes: Measure results, not hours worked

### ❌ DON'T:
- Micromanage: Trust your team and give them autonomy
- Ignore team dynamics: Address conflicts and issues early
- Skip 1:1s: Regular check-ins are critical for engagement and performance
- Play favorites: Treat all team members fairly and equitably
- Avoid difficult conversations: Address performance and behavioral issues directly
- Take credit: Recognize and celebrate team achievements publicly
- Neglect development: Team growth should be a continuous priority
- Be inconsistent: Your team needs predictability and reliability

---

## Common Mistakes

1. **Not setting clear expectations**: Ambiguity leads to misalignment and frustration
2. **Providing feedback only during reviews**: Continuous feedback is more effective
3. **Ignoring team health**: Monitor engagement, burnout, and collaboration quality
4. **Taking on too much**: Delegate and empower team members
5. **Being too hands-off**: Some level of guidance and support is always needed
6. **Neglecting development**: Team members need growth opportunities to stay engaged
7. **Not addressing toxic behavior**: One toxic team member can destroy team culture
8. **Focusing only on tasks**: Relationships, culture, and dynamics matter too

## Example Workflows

### Workflow 1: Setting Up a New Team
1. Define team mission, vision, and values (team charter)
2. Create clear role descriptions and expectations
3. Establish team operating agreements and working norms
4. Set up regular meeting cadence and communication channels
5. Design onboarding process for new team members
6. Create initial performance goals and metrics
7. Launch team and monitor team health
8. Iterate and refine based on feedback

### Workflow 2: Scaling an Existing Team
1. Assess current team performance and dynamics
2. Identify growth areas and skill gaps
3. Create organizational design for larger team
4. Define new roles and hire accordingly
5. Onboard new team members with buddy system
6. Establish sub-teams or squads if needed
7. Update team processes and rituals for scale
8. Monitor team health and adjust as needed

### Workflow 3: Turning Around a Struggling Team
1. Assess team health and identify root causes
2. Gather 1:1 feedback from all team members
3. Address toxic behaviors or performance issues directly
4. Re-establish team charter and operating agreements
5. Set clear expectations and performance goals
6. Provide support and coaching where needed
7. Celebrate small wins to rebuild morale
8. Monitor progress and adjust approach
