### Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Distribute to 3-5 platforms maximum (focus over breadth)
- Optimize for each platform's unique format
- Respond to comments in first 24 hours
- Cross-link between platforms strategically
- Track distribution metrics per platform

❌ **Avoid:**
- Posting identical content on all platforms
- Ignoring platform-specific best practices
- Forgetting to engage after publishing
- Overloading schedule (quality over quantity)
- Sharing once and forgetting (evergreen content needs ongoing promotion)
