# Editorial Calendar

Plans, schedules, and manages content production with editorial calendars, deadline tracking, multi-platform coordination, and workflow automation. Essential for content marketers, creators, and teams producing consistent content at scale.

## When This Skill Activates

- You mention "editorial calendar", "content schedule", or "publishing plan"
- You ask to "plan content" or "schedule posts"
- You're managing content production for multiple platforms
- You need to track content deadlines and deliverables
- You want to automate content publishing workflows

**Available after session 23**

At this level, I help you create a basic editorial calendar and schedule content production.

1. **Calendar Setup**

### Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Plan content 2-4 weeks in advance
- Balance content pillars and formats
- Build buffer time for unexpected delays
- Track content performance systematically
- Use templates to speed up workflow

❌ **Avoid:**
- Scheduling content day-by-day (no buffer)
- Overcommitting on publishing frequency
- Ignoring content performance data
- Skipping content reviews
- Publishing without SEO optimization

- Automating everything at once (too risky)
- Overlooking manual review steps
- Forgetting to monitor automation performance
- Building automations without error handling
