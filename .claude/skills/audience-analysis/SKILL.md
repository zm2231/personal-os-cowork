# Audience Analysis

Analyzes audience demographics, behaviors, preferences, and engagement patterns to create data-driven content and marketing strategies. Essential for content marketers, creators, and businesses seeking to understand and grow their audience.

## When This Skill Activates

- You mention "audience analysis", "target audience", or "demographics"
- You ask to "analyze my audience" or "understand my readers"
- You're researching audience preferences or behaviors
- You need to segment audiences for targeted content
- You want to optimize content for audience engagement

### Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Use real data for personas (don't guess)
- Update personas quarterly with new data
- Validate assumptions with surveys
- Create content for each persona
- Track engagement by persona

❌ **Avoid:**
- Creating personas without research
- Assuming all audience members are the same
- Ignoring minority audience segments
- Never updating outdated personas
- Overgeneralizing based on limited data

### Best Practices
